If you do not insert your card, instructions for using audio guided transactions will follow.

The card reader is located three inches to the right of the keypad.

The keypad is located seven inches below the audio jack, and three inches to the left.

The keypad is arranged in telephone style with the one key in the upper left.

The five key has a raised dot on it.

The cancel key has a raised X symbol on it and is located at the top of the right most column of keys.
The clear key has a raised less than symbol on it and is below the cancel key.
The enter key has a raised circle symbol on it and is below the clear key.

Pressing the key to the right of the zero key will increase the volume.
Pressing the key to the left of the zero key will decrease the volume.

Pressing the key below the enter key will repeat the instructions for the screen.

Press the cancel key to cancel a transaction and then remove your headset when instructed.

The cash is dispensed through a shutter that is approximately 3 inches to the left of the keypad.

The receipt is presented six inches above the audio jack and slightly to the right.

All transactions can be completed using only the keypad.

To begin, please insert your card and remove your card.
